Lifetide is a people’s movement for water sustainability, justice and harmony.

Members of Lifetide come from various backgrounds and belief systems. While we share a common vision of crafting a culture of living mindfully, we might have varied opinions on approaches and/or methodology... our discussions are around the structure of the city, how it supports the natural environment and how we choose to live. The value that bind people are.

  • Love of water.
  • Belief in strength of community
  • Respect for individual expression
  • Responsibility to protect watersheds.
  • The knowing that mother nature doesn't need us.
